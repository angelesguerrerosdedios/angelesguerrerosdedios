Who We Are

We are a non-profit foundation, willing to work hand in hand with the community and with some entities that want to help us, either to obtain funds to buy school kits or to receive donations from them as such, to take to the children. in order to motivate them to go to classes and help eradicate school dropouts due to lack of school supplies.

Our Impact

We have visited 10 municipalities in Magdalena, where we have taken school kits to 18 most vulnerable public schools, benefiting more than 8,000 children in 10 years of service to the community.
